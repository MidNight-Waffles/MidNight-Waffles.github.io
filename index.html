Welcome to Our App

This app is based on Mark Reiter, who runs the Code Day Chicago event, and his awesome Waffle making skills. He makes his waffles beautifully and he makes them at Midnight.

Designing the Game

The app was created using Java. The characters, and enemies, were created using Photo Shop. It took a lot of time to think of ideas needed to make it a quality game with a lot of items, power ups, and power downs. We had a team of 6 with each person doing a different part of the Project. Sometimes 2-3 people worked on one part, but then went back to doing their own thing to help the project, again.
